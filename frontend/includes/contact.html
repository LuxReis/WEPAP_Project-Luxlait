OUR HISTORY

The origins of Luxlait go back to 1894, which is marked by the foundation of the “Central-Molkerei” in Luxembourg City. This dairy quickly became a public limited company and changed its name to “Zentralmolkerei”. The structure of Luxlait today has gradually developed and was particularly marked during the Second World War.
